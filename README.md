## 🧠 The Vision
HireNest is not a CRM. It is a **Self-Operating Recruitment Company**. It transforms the traditional human-heavy recruitment agency model into a digital, autonomous workforce. It combines high-intent signal discovery with neural candidate matching and automated sales execution.

---

## 🏗️ Technical Architecture (Architect View)

### 1. Neural Intelligence Layer
*   **The Brain (Nestor):** Orchestrates multiple sub-agents using a custom "Routing Engine" powered by Gemini 1.5.
*   **Vector Pipeline:** Real-time semantic matching between multi-dimensional job requisitions and candidate profiles.
*   **Autonomous Logic:** Deterministic workflow triggers combined with non-deterministic AI decisions.

### 2. Execution Layer (Nodes)
*   **Ingestion Node:** High-speed Gmail & Document processing for automated resume parsing and lead capture.
*   **Communication Node:** Omni-channel outreach via Gmail and WhatsApp Business protocols.
*   **Agent Intelligence:** A command center for monitoring agent decisions, execution health, and "GPU-to-Revenue" metrics.

### 3. Data & Persistence
*   **Infrastructure:** Scalable Supabase (PostgreSQL) backend with real-time Row-Level Security (RLS).
*   **Event Logging:** Atomic logging of every AI decision to ensure transparency and "Explainable AI" for agency owners.

---

## 💼 The Workflow (Admin/Owner View)

### 1. Discovery
The **Scout Agent** scans incoming data streams (Email/Ads/Inbounds) to identify "High-Intent" hiring signals. It filters the noise and surfaces only real revenue opportunities.

### 2. Autonomous Matching
The **Match Agent** instantly scans your talent pool. If a match exists, it doesn't just notify you—it prepares the outreach.

### 3. Smart Execution
The **Communication Agent** initiates dialogue. It understands context, handles basic questions, and books meetings while the human team sleeps.

### 4. Revenue Insights
The **Revenue Hub** tracks exactly how much value the AI is generating. It measures "Human Hours Saved" and "Value Captured," turning your tech stack into a P&L center.

## 🛡️ Security & Scalability
*   **Multi-Tenancy:** Hardened database isolation for agency-level privacy.
*   **Rate Limiting:** Managed AI throughput to prevent resource exhaustion.
*   **Human-in-the-loop:** Strategic handoff points where AI escalates complex negotiations to human experts.
